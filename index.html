About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your goal is to stay alive as long as possible while facing endless waves of monsters. As you progress, you'll collect experience points to level up and unlock powerful abilities that will help you survive longer.

With its roguelike elements and progressive difficulty system, each playthrough offers a unique experience. Choose your upgrades carefully and create powerful combinations to overcome increasingly challenging enemies.
